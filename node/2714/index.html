Milord Arsouille, 2nd chapter, The Infernal Machine, No. 5. Having made a terrible discovery, Nina ran to and confided in Maria Benares, whom she found with Lord Seymour. - What happened to you, my child? - I recognize you sir, you saved me; I am glad I can thank you. I discovered in the desk of my step-father, who has been absent for a few days … the plan of the infernal machine, and the address of a certain Pepin in Les Lilas. - Maybe this man will know what happened to Jacques Montbrun … we must investigate. - Little one, we will help you find your fiance. To make his project go well, Lord Seymour called on Aristide of whom Nina has spoken. - First of all we have to deal with Pepin, but I hesitate to make this kid a part of it. - I'm not afraid, Mister! - He's very capable, he'll be useful …. Touched by Nina's distress, Lord Seymour offered her and her mother his hospitality. - Why this sudden departure to an unknown house? We are threatened here by grave dangers, few will be safe …. You are not telling me the truth ... my husband … died? - No mother, calm down! Watch the end in No. 6.
