Abstract: 
Universal Review of the Sciences, Arts, Industries, Travels and Sports presented by Pathé-Baby. The beautiful corners of France: The Lot around Cahors. The rock of Luzech overlooked by its old tower built in the 13th century. The city of Cahors is surrounded by the loop of Lot. The silhouette of the old towers are the last remains of the former palace. The Barcane is an interesting structure of the middle ages that served as a guard at the gate of the city. The Bridge of Valentré, a one of a kind monument. It's a marvelous fortified structure whose construction dates back to 1308. The tower of Pendus inside or which gallows were installed. The graceful feline. Our friends, the cats. Some animals that were awarded at the last "Cat Club of France" show. Siamese cats. A pretty basket. Industry: Manufacturing lady bags. The pattern is established…then it is cut into the fabric. Mounting and seaming. A pretty model of a modern bag. Hungarian Outfits: A Mesokovesd. In this village, it is common to wear national outfits each Sunday. The old woman wears a black dress ... And the men, an embroidered coat. Leaving Mass. The beautiful embroidery garnishes the dresses of these young girls. Abd El Krim in exile. At the meeting when he was exiled, the formerhead of the Riffians leads a quiet life, without suspicion. Here he is in a huge park of the castle that was allocated to him by the French government. Rogui's children accompany him in his exile.
