Abstract: 
Surcouf (2nd Chapter), The English Pontoons, No. 2. - Why don't you want to be my wife? - Because I don't love you! You always think about Robert … you're waiting for him in vain. Offshore, the ship "La Confiance," is signaled. - This is Robert's ship. An English pontoon, the "Crown." Down in steerage, to fight against boredom, the French prisoners engaged in various jobs. "Surcouf, my friend, I am a prisonner aboard an English Pontoon called the 'Crown.' Come and free me. Marcof." Surcouf informed his parents about his return, and his plans. - So, do you want to marry this woman. - If you only knew how wonderful and beautiful she is. - Well then! Go and find her!
