Abstract: 
Milord Arsouille, 1st chapter, Nina, No. 5. The next day. - I heard about yesterday, and I had to see you before going to my newspaper. - Rest assured Jacques, I love you and shall be your wife. Fieschi updated his accomplices. - Nina shall consent and it shall be taken care of. Nina loves Jacques Montbrun, as you know. I have a plan, since Jacques Montbrun is bothering my plans…. - Why should I care about this scribbler? Any hesitation is fatal to our plan. The three companions each pursued a clever plan. Jacques Montbrun, editor of the "Republican Monitor". - What are you doing here so late? - I got confused, I'm on the wrong floor. Watch the end in No. 6.
