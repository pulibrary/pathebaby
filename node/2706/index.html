Mylord L'Arsouille (1er Chap) Nina, No. 3.

Title: Mylord L'Arsouille (1er Chap) Nina, No. 3.
Translated Title: 
Milord Arsouille (1st Chapter) Nina, No. 3.
Abstract: 
Milord Arsouille, 1st chapter, Nina, No. 3. Maria Benares manged to enter the private room in the cabaret. - Who is this young woman? You abducted her! I command you to let her go! At this moment a mysterious stranger …. - Let this young maiden part! - You! In an underground passage, Fieschi engaged in a mysterious activity, accompanied by his friends Pepin and Morey. - There will be thirteen cannons charged with grapeshot, enough to mow down an entire company. - Yes! it is on the 28th of July that the King will review troops on the Boulevard du Temple. He'll pass under the windows of an apartment we rented. The next day Benares was visited by the mysterious stranger. - You, sir? - I would like to meet the young girl you saved, to prevent a scandal for … Lord Seymour. - Her name is Nina Fieschi; may I ask you why you are so interested in Lord Seymour? I am Lord Seymour! Milord Arsouille is an impostor who has a vendetta against my family. He has letters that could dishonor my family. - The mother of Milord Arsouille, Irma Rosier, who was a companion of my mother's, made a great error, which she attributed to my father …. Milord Arsouille made his fortune in America, and since his return he has dragged the name Seymour in the mud, as a way of avenging his mother. - Now you know my sad fate! ... Still haunted by the memory of Nina, Milord Arsouille came to discover Fieschi underground. - Don't go, we must talk! Watch more in No. 4.
