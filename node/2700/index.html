Nène, No. 9 (continuation). From the next day, the threat was clear. The cost was that Michel Corbier fired her; after three years of devotion to the happiness for the children … and maybe another thing that wasn't forgotten …. - As for you Madeleine, I can no longer keep you, on All Saints' the kids will go to school and I will no longer need you. Jean was also marked by misery; he tilted his head a bit like an invalid who doesn't dare look life in the face. Boisseriot enjoyed getting himself drunk, knowing that while he was sweet and sensible sober, he would be belligerent after drinking. - Today you should buy an engagement ring, and they spent the afternoon together, and at the end of the day Corbier returned to the farm. It was true, Violette said yes, it was security for her, a monotonous route perhaps, but it would be easy from time to time to get away with a bit of feeling …. Watch more in No. 10.
