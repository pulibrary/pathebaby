Abstract: 
Zoology. The armadillo. It is a mammalian type of creature of the toothless family inhabiting the southern region of the Americas. The armadillo is remarkable because of its scaly shell. This shell consists of a shield covering the forehead and another one protecting the rump. Moving plaques uniformly arranged into crosscutting bands cover the entire back region. The tail is covered along its entire length with rings of scales. The very thick skin of the belly is covered in fine hairs. The armadillo lives in small groups in the woods and in the plains. They dig burrows to shelter themselves from big carnivores. Funny experiences. How to knot a scarf with one hand. Place the scarf in the hand between the thumb and index finger and between the ring finger and pinky finger. Turn the hand and grasp one of the ends of the scarf with the index, middle, and ring fingers. Continue to pull the end held with those three fingers. One swift motion and the knot is made. Pathe-baby interviews Mrs. Robinne and Mr. Alexandre of the French Comedy. Mrs. Robinne at 6 years old. Mrs. Robinne is 15 when she first enters Conservatory. At 17 years old, Mrs. Robinne debuts at the French Comedy in " Le Passant" in the role of Zanetto. Here she is playing a scene from " Misanthrope" accompanied by her husband Mr. Alexandre. Mr. Alexandre has been with the French Comedy since 1908 where he has created numerous roles. Here he is in some works from the repertoire of Berenice. Oedipus. Le Flibustier. Sports. Golf. Golf lovers have a surprising dexterity. Industry. Nacre work. The Nacre is found in a certain shell called the mother of pearl. The objects are produced in the shell. The grindstone gets rid of the coarse exterior of the shell. Then skilled craftsman chisel and sculpt from the piece of nacre obtained from the prior process. All kinds of objects are crafted from nacre. Travel. Medenine. Medenine is a major city in the far south of Tunisia. Some native people. The dwellings made from compressed mud are stacked like the honeycombs of bees. Access to and exit from the highest dwellings are hardly practical. If you would like to collect news from the entire world, subscribe to Pathe-Gazette. Pathe- Gazette, live journal of news from the whole world served to you at your home for a modest price. Subscription rates for France (one film every 15 days). One year … 130 francs. Six months … 70 francs (postal check or money order.)
