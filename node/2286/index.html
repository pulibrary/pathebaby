Pathe-Magazine Revue Universelle des Sciences, Arts, Industries, Voyages, & Sports, No. 38

Title: Pathe-Magazine Revue Universelle des Sciences, Arts, Industries, Voyages, & Sports, No. 38
Translated Title: 
Pathe-Magazine, Universal Magazine of the Sciences, Arts, Industry, Travel, and Sports, No. 38
Duration: 
04:23
Abstract: 
The sites of France. Tours. The celebrated vineyards of Vouvray and the Loire valley. A road of cavedwellers in Vouvray. A villa dug into the chalk. The curious lantern of Rochecorbon remains with a lordly medieval demeanor. The house of Tristan Ermite. An old road in Tours. The Judicial Palace. The City Hotel. The Art of Mechanics. Automated Mannequins. The body of the mannequin is first modeled by the artist then molded in a plaster mold. A complicated mechanism gives the impression of life. The anatomy of the smoker. The machine produces smoke. A perfected subject. Zoology. The porcupine. The porcupine is a mammal rodent living in the temperate regions of the two continents. It nourishes itself from roots and from the bark of trees that it climbs with ease. The burrow that it digs. Its feet are armed with robust claws. These types of extremely pointy needles… are for it a sure line of defense. Beautiful Gardens. A modern rose nursery. A harmoniously disposed amphitheatre where the reign of flowers flourishes. A flowering bunch of climbing roses. In June and July the rose garden is enchanting for promenaders. All the variety of roses in all their forms. The charm of blooming and perfumed paths. A pergola. Aviation. Air acrobats. Fearless aviators with parachutes jump out of airplanes during flight. The slow motion shot allows us to follow the movements of this daring descent.
